### 3. "How Komplai Helps" (Core Features)
*Ref: Screenshot .03.18 / .03.22*

*   **Section Title:** How Komplai Helps
*   **Feature 01: Real-Time Transaction Matching**
    *   **Description:** Match bank lines to ledger entries using advanced rules and historical patterns. Reduce manual tagging and the tedious repetitive cleanup work that usually piles up until month-end.
*   **Feature 02: Smart Handling of Partials and Payouts**
    *   **Description:** Group split settlements, partial receipts, payouts, fees, and refunds automatically. Turn messy payment provider statements (Stripe/PayPal) into one explainable, perfectly balanced match.

    *   **Description:** Surface duplicates, timing reminders, and missing postings early. Larry suggests fixes and identifies required backup so owners can resolve issues and close fast.

    *   **Description:** Capture reviewer approval, timestamps, and direct source links for every reconciliation. Build enterprise-grade internal controls without the need for extra manual checklists.
