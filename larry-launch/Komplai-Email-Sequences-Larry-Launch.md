# APPENDIX: COLD EMAIL PRINCIPLES APPLIED

These emails follow **PAS** and **BAB** — the most widely cited B2B cold email structures.
Key principles applied:

1. **Personalize the opener.** Email 1 opens with `{{personalization_sentence}}` — a researched sentence about the recipient. This is the single highest-leverage change for reply rates.
2. **Ultra-short:** Research shows 50-125 words performs best. All outbound emails target this range.
3. **Lead with their world.** Open with a moment the reader recognizes. Not a product feature.
4. **No links, reply only:** All outbound emails use reply as the only CTA. No URLs in the email body — this prevents Gmail spam classification.
5. **Threaded social proof:** One consistent company story (Series B SaaS, 2-person finance team) runs across Emails 2-5, building familiarity and credibility through repetition.
6. **Learning positioning:** Emails 1-2 frame Rishi as wanting to learn from the reader's experience. Email 3 positions Larry as built from those conversations. Emails 4-5 invite comparison.
7. **Quantifiable value in every email:** Every email contains at least one specific number — "8 out of 10," "28 days," "12 minutes," "10 seconds," "$47K."
8. **No SaaS marketing language:** "I built" not "we launched." "Business leaders" not "finance leaders." Outcomes not features. Tangible not aspirational.
