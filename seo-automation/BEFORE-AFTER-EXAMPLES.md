## üìù Writing Style Transformation


**Issues:**
- Every sentence starts differently (forced rule)
- Excessive transitions ("Furthermore", "Moreover", "Additionally")

- Corporate jargon ("transformative approach", "methodology")
- No personality

```
exhausting. But here's what 200+ finance teams have found: continuous close actually
```

- ‚úÖ Conversational opener ("Here's the thing")
