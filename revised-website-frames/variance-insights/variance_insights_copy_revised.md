### 4. Strategic Benefits
*Ref: Screenshot .05.46 / .05.49*

*   **Section Title:** One system to monitor, review, and report.
*   **Pillar 01: Faster variance reviews, fewer meetings**
    *   **Description:** Teams stop spending hours just locating the driver of a variance. Larry ranks by $ materiality and shows the underlying entries instantly. Most teams cut variance review cycles by 50–70%.
*   **Pillar 02: Cleaner numbers through mapping and anomaly detection**
    *   **Description:** When "Unknown" or misclassified ledgers drive top movements, Komplai surfaces it early. Finance teams typically reduce misclass-related noise by 20–40% over just a few closes.
*   **Pillar 03: Better decision-making because insights arrive earlier**
    *   **Description:** When finance can explain movements quickly, leadership doesn't have to wait for "final numbers" to act. Teams get usable insights days earlier, not after the books are closed.
