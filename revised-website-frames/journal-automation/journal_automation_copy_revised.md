    *   **Description:** Generate entries from operational triggers and schedules. Standardise narration, tags, and attachments automatically, ensuring every entry is "Review-Ready" from the second it's drafted.
*   **Feature 02: Auto Posting to the General Ledger**
    *   **Description:** Push approved journals into your ERP with consistent metadata. Keep references clean so audits and reviews don't stall. Move from manual data entry to one-click verification.
*   **Feature 03: Validation and Accuracy Checks**
    *   **Description:** Catch mapping gaps, amount mismatches, missing fields, and wrong periods before they hit your books. Fail early, fix once, and maintain absolute sub-ledger integrity.

    *   **Description:** Every entry carries its original source data, approval logs, and edit history. Cut audit prep time dramatically with 100% traceability for every dollar moved.
